About DriveSafe AI

DriveSafe AI is a hands-free driver alert system that uses advanced AI vision technology to detect drowsiness, distraction, and impairment in real-time. The app continuously monitors the driver through their device's camera and provides immediate visual and audio alerts when signs of unsafe driving are detected.

Built with a focus on road safety, DriveSafe AI aims to reduce accidents caused by driver fatigue, distraction, and impairment. The system uses OpenAI's GPT-4 Vision API to analyze facial expressions, eye movements, and body language to determine the driver's state and alert them before dangerous situations occur.

🔴 Impairment Detection

Detects signs of alcohol or substance impairment with red visual alerts

🟠 Drowsiness Monitoring

Identifies fatigue and sleepiness with orange overlay warnings

🟡 Distraction Alerts

Monitors attention and focus with yellow alerts for distracted driving

📱 Real-Time Analysis

Continuous monitoring with analysis every 3 seconds for immediate feedback

🔔 Multi-Modal Alerts

Visual overlays, browser notifications, and audio alerts for maximum awareness

📲 PWA Support

Installable as a Progressive Web App for easy access on any device
